Common breeds

Devon Rex

Active, playful, and social. The Devon Rex is a small but mighty cat with a friendly temperament. Devon Rexes love to cuddle up against humans and other pets in their family.

American Shorthair

gentle, affectionate, playful, easygoing, and curious. These cats are known for their amiable personalities. They get along very well in homes with children, other cats or cat friendly dogs. but they don't demand constant attention. Your American Shorthair can entertain itself with toys, spend time gazing out the window, clamber up and down a cat tree, or enjoy a good catnap when not spending time cuddling or interacting with you.

British Shorthair

Affectionate, easy-going, and calm. The British shorthair is a wonderful family cat—the breed has an easy-going personality and generally gets along with everyone, including kids and other animals living in the house. They are active without being hyper, tend to be quiet, and can live happily in almost any size house, including apartments.

Burmese

If dogs are more your speed, don't count out the boisterous Burmese highly social and craving companionship, they are often described as dog-like. These cats don't like to be left alone for long periods of time and enjoy stimulating environments with their people, complete with toys, window access, and perhaps even a friendly game of fetch.

Manx

The Manx cat is a unique-looking, affectionate, friendly cat. Manx owners love their cat's stubby tail and high trainability. They are known to hunt rodents and insects with precision, but their hunting instincts don't translate to an aggressive temperament toward humans. These cats are excellent jumpers, which may make for some frustration, but remember that the Manx cat's playful nature is a gift. The Manx cat has a wonderful personality and makes a great addition to any family.

Singapura

Energetic, intelligent, social The Singapura has a very social personality. loves to be involved in your daily activities, and is happiest when they are with family. Singapuras thrive on company, both human and animal—including dogs when introduced at an early age. As a result, they do not do well when left alone for long periods.

Tonkinese

Friendly , playful, intelligent, active, and intelligent, the Tonkinese loves to play, but its easygoing temperament makes it content to spend time with its family without a lot of attention or stimulation. It is happy to "speak" when spoken to but doesn't vocalize excessively. The Tonk has an irrepressible people-loving personality. It is eager to be pet, held, talked to, played with, and cuddled on a warm lap.

Europian Shorthair

Intelligent and exceptional hunters, While loyal and affectionate, they also maintained an independent, instinctive lifestyle.They are well suited to domestic life, ideally with access to the outdoors so they can channel their instinctive hunting traits.

Ragdoll

Friendly, easygoing, cuddly, social. With its easygoing personality and social temperament, the ragdoll is ready to be friends with just about anyone. The ragdoll does not need a lot of exercise or rambunctious play time, but snuggle time is essential.

Bombay

Bombays are active, curious cats and adapt very easily to change. At any age, they love to look at the world around them and their favorite place may often turn out to be a window where they can observe the world outside. These cats are very affectionate and will, at times, demand time with their parents.

Bengal

Affectionate, energetic, animated are beautiful, wild-looking, and smart cats. The Bengal is affectionate, playful, and can get along with people of all ages as well as other pets. It is an energetic cat and likes room to play..

Oriental Shorthair

Affectionate, playful, sociable, and vocalThis cat is outgoing and fun.gregarious by nature and, unlike many other breeds, can become withdrawn and depressed when left alone for more than a few hours at a time.
